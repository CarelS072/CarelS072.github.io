Reflections of chapter 6 - Search Inside Yourself

Passion - The passion is getting fed. Learning some new skills which I can hone into "building" skills is something I am looking forward to.
Purpose - Not there, I am not doing what I am doing to change the world. I will be helpfull towards my surroundings and clean up where I can.
