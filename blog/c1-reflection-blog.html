    • What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

    In the very beginning before I have seen Shereef's video and visited EDC, Auckland, I was a bit skeptical on the program. But having spoken spoken some of its members and viewing Shereef's video I understand that I should not view it as a "course" or "university" but more like an experience towards a foundation to build upon.

    What are your impressions?

    Good, I am looking forward on the first day of the bootcamp and what I will learn from it

    How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

    Take the experience in day-by-day and be respectfull of others

    Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

    No, my expectations haven't but my impressions have. I like to know how something got to the place/time it currently is, in order to know how it will progress and move on.

    Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

    Yes, I am very excited and rather then nervous I am anxious. Knowing that this is the first step navigating into a different role and knowing that I am able to stand together with others in a similar goals makes me feel enpowered.
